Our Process: From Scope to Site Quality

We follow a rigorous, 10-step process to ensure transparency, efficiency, and maximum value at every stage of the procurement lifecycle.

1

Project Initiation

Engage with the technical team to ensure all project details and specifications are available.

2

Document Verification

Verify all assumptions, supporting documents, and initial budget estimates.

3

RFQ Preparation

Identify all required items, finalize quantities, and include necessary safety factors.

4

Vendor Outreach

Prepare and send detailed, comprehensive RFQs (Request for Quotation) with clear scope and specifications.

5

Database Mapping

Map item requirements to our Mega Vendor Database to ensure comprehensive coverage.

6

Price Breakup

Send comprehensive RFQs demanding itemized price breakups, not lump-sum quotes.

7

Target Setting

Post-initial quotes, circulate calculated target prices to drive competitive bidding.

8

Finalization

Evaluate qualitative (technical fit) & commercial factors, and finalize the optimal vendor.

9

Contract Standardization

Standardize T&C (Terms & Conditions) for selected contracts and materials.

10

Quality Assurance

Final quality check via the client’s technical team and 3rd party lab testing where applicable.
